2017-10-30

Last two days have shown that:
  • AWS does not provide computational resources in terms of the free account which I need for my test cluster
  • those free testing VMs were not connecting in a Docker cluster and just way too slow
  • a local physical setup of four machines renders a private local cloud with 25 cores and 50 GB RAM

Following the OSI model, I had to dig into almost forgotten activities - change hard drives, burn OS installation media and debug networking issues.

Now, first swarm-wider deployed service converged, and there is even a local (insecure) registry.
